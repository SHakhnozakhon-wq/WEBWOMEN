Khan Academy

Khan Academy is a global online learning platform offering free educational resources in various subjects, including math, science, and humanities.

Sololearn

SoloLearn is a popular online learning platform designed for individuals interested in learning coding and programming. It offers a wide range of courses in various programming languages, including Python, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, SQL, and more. SoloLearn is known for its interactive learning experience, combining bite-sized lessons, quizzes, and coding challenges to reinforce learning. The platform also has a strong community aspect, allowing users to engage in discussions, share their projects, and participate in coding competitions.

Coder

Coder.com is a cloud-based development platform that allows developers to create, manage, and deploy code from anywhere with an internet connection. It provides a powerful, browser-based integrated development environment (IDE) that supports multiple programming languages and frameworks. Coder enables developers to work on their projects remotely, eliminating the need for local development environments. It also offers collaborative features, allowing teams to work together in real-time on the same codebase.
